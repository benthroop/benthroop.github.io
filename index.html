MIGS 2017 Talk - Blowing Things up in Unity

November 5, 2020

I was invited to speak at MIGS in Montreal in 2017 and gave a talk there. It was a bit of a surprise that they weren't recording the talks, but I was able to cobble together a recording of it anyway.

I went up to Montreal a few days early and got an AirBnB to prep the talk from. I actually liked this approach because I could really concentrate, but it was a bit stressful crunching it together at the last minute.

I mention in the talk that I would post this code to Github, but never got around to doing that. If you are interested hit me up on the contact form and I'll send it to you.
