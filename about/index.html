About

My last name is pronounced TROOP. The "Th" is like in "Thomas" or "Thames". I grew up in Rochester, NY.

I make video games and have been in the industry for over 20 years now. I run Frame Interactive in Burlington, VT, USA. I used to be a Game Director at Vicarious Visions/Activision and before that was at Sony Bend Studio in Oregon.

I have been building games with Unity for over 10 years. I had a similar affair with 3DSMax which started in 1996 and ended with the release of Blender 2.8. 

I look at game making as a inherently multi-disciplinary act, to the extent that it is an art form itself. So while I write code, model, animate, and texture in 3d, do graphic design, do audio, and last but not least design gameplay, I consider it all an integrative pursuit.

So really, I'm just a gamemaker.

You can find me on Twitter and Discord if you want to talk back. I have a LinkedIn profile. 

Feel free to email me at (ben at frameinteractive.com), or you can use the contact form.
