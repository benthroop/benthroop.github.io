About

I make video games using Unity. I used to be a Game Director at Vicarious Visions/Activision and before that was at Sony Bend Studio in Oregon. Now I run Frame Interactive in Burlington, VT, USA. Sometimes I teach at Champlain College.

After 20+ years of working on the variety of skills needed to make games, at this point I'm comfortable saying I'm an all around gamemaker. Some days I'm programming, some days I'm making art, sound, or other content. Most of the time I'm thinking about what I'm working on and not often enough, I'm writing it all down here.

You can find me on Twitter and Discord. I have a LinkedIn profile. And of course feel free to email me at (ben at frameinteractive.com), or you can use the contact form.
