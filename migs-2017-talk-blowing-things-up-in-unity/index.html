MIGS 2017 Talk - Blowing Things up in Unity

November 5, 2020

I was invited to speak at MIGS in Montreal in 2017 and gave a talk there. It was a bit of a surprise that they weren't recording the talks, but I was able to cobble together a recording of it anyway.

This trip was pretty interesting. I went up to Montreal a few days early and got an AirBnB to prep the talk from. I actually liked this approach because I could really concentrate, but it was a bit stressful crunching it together at the last minute.

Hilariously, my approach was to do a live demo! Did I mention it was stressful to prep? Well the demo itself went well with only one near disaster when I accidentally deleted an essential file in Unity that broke compilation. Somehow my subconscious detected the error and I was able to backtrack and fix it, but for a minute I was dead in the water.
